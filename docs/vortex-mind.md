## 2. Generating a Report

To generate a new insight report, navigate to the **Generate a Report** tab and follow these steps:

1.  **Connect a Store:** Select the store you wish to analyze from your connected sources.
2.  **Configure Modules:** Select which AI agents to include (Introduction, KPI Analysis, Competitor Analysis, etc.).
3.  **Set Parameters:** Define target markets, dates, and prompt preferences for each selected module.
4.  **Generate:** Click **Generate Now** to begin the report creation process.

## 3. Viewing and Scheduling

### Reviewing Completed Reports
The **View Reports** tab allows you to track the generation status of all reports. You can view, download, or search through your manually generated history.

### Automated Reporting (Schedules)
Use the **Scheduled Reports** tab to automate your data insights:

* **Create New Schedule:** Define the report name, store, and template.
* **Set Frequency:** Choose between "Weekdays at 8 AM", "Daily at midnight", "Weekly", or "Monthly".
* **Delivery Channels:** Configure where the report should be sent, including **Email**, **Slack**, or **MS Teams**.
* **Podcast Version:** Optionally toggle "Automatically generate podcast version" to receive an audio summary of your data.

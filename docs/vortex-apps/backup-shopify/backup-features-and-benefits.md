# Features and Benefits

Here’s a breakdown of this app’s features and their associated benefits:

## **Features**:

### Realtime Backup
  - Means the VIQ Backup app is set to actively listen to the BigCommerce store webhooks and tracks changes in real-time.    
  - It is done for these data items: Products, Categories, Customers, Orders, Channels, Pages, Email Templates.
    
### Manual Backup
  - It is applicable for data entities which don’t have webhooks, so you can take full backups by clicking on ‘**Backup**’ button.
  - It can done for these data items: Brands, Promotions, Blog Posts, Coupons, Gift Certificates, 301 redirects.
    
### Version Tracking
  - Tracks changes to your store, providing a complete history of updates and backups. Allows you to restore to any previous version safely and securely.

### Quick Data Recovery
  - Restores from the last good backup with minimal downtime on BigCommerce. Minimises disruptions to business operations and ensures a seamless customer experience.

### Ease of Use
  - Provides a simple web interface for managing backup and restore tasks on the BigCommerce platform. Requires no technical expertise, making it accessible for all users.

## **Benefits**:

### Peace of Mind
  - Automates data protection, ensuring your BigCommerce stores are always backed up and ready for recovery.
	
### Increased Efficiency
  - Real-time backups saves time and operational effort for your team.
	
### Business Continuity
  - Minimises downtime with quick and accurate restoration, keeping your operations running smoothly across platforms.

### Data Security
  - Uses advanced protection methods to safeguard your data and configurations on BigCommerce.

### Customer Confidence
  - Enhances trust by ensuring your stores remain secure and operational, protecting your reputation.

### Adaptability
  - Works seamlessly with other AI agents to provide a comprehensive store management ecosystem.

## Selective Content Migration: Pages and Layouts

This feature allows you to migrate specific **Pages (Custom Pages)** and **Layouts (Page Templates)** between different storefronts within your StagingPro application.

To perform a selective migration of pages & layouts:

 1. Select the source store and storefront.
 2. Select the destination store and storefront.
 3. Choose the pages or layouts you want to migrate.
 4. Click the Copy Pages button to begin the migration.

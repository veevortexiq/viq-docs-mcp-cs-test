#StagingPro

Welcome to the StagingPro Wiki, where you will find many articles on how best to use StagingPro or to undertake any required troubleshooting.

StagingPro simplifies staging with automated workflows, real-time migration monitoring, and secure data handling. 

Its seamless integration with GitHub and other AI agents ensures efficient deployments and team collaboration. With features like selective migration, scheduled rollouts, and rollback support, it optimises operations while reducing errors and saving valuable time.

Whether you are a store owner or an agency with dozens of team members, StagingPro has been designed to get you up and running within minutes. 

Refer to [StagingPro Onboarding](stagingpro-onboarding/index.md) as reference to quickly get started.

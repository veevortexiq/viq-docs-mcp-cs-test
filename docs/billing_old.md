## 2. Usage & Limits

Since Vortex IQ is an AI Operating System, your plan includes specific allowances for system resources. You can track your consumption here in real-time.

### Common Usage Metrics:
* **Signals Processed:** The volume of data events analyzed by the Nerve Centre.
* **Active Agents:** The number of autonomous AI agents currently deployed.
* **Storage:** Data retention limits for logs and backups.

<p style="font-size: 0.9rem; color: #475569; margin-top: 1rem;"><em>Note: If you approach 100% of your limit, you will receive a notification to upgrade to prevent service interruption.</em></p>

## 3. Payment & Invoices

View your payment method and download historical invoices for accounting purposes.

### Updating Payment Method
<div style="background: #f8fafc; padding: 1.5rem; border-radius: 6px; border: 1px solid #e2e8f0;">
<p>1. Go to the <strong>Payment Method</strong> section.</p>
<p>2. Click <strong>Update Card</strong>.</p>
<p>3. Enter your new credit card details securely via Stripe.</p>
</div>

### Downloading Invoices
All past invoices are listed by date. Click the **Download PDF** icon next to any line item to save a copy for your records.

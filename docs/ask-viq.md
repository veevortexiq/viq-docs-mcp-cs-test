# Getting Started with Ask Viq

Learn how to utilize Ask Viq, the conversational AI assistant for Vortex IQ, to query business data, deploy specialized agents, and automate operations.

## Overview

<h3>Your 24/7 Data Operations Partner</h3><p><strong>Ask Viq</strong> is the conversational AI engine integrated into the <span style="color: #4f46e5; font-weight: 800;">Vortex IQ Operating System</span>. It functions as a knowledgeable colleague available around the clock, capable of understanding your specific business context and providing instant answers.</p><p>Instead of manually exporting CSVs, building spreadsheets, or interpreting raw dashboards, Ask Viq allows you to query your data in plain English. It is powered by advanced AI but strictly grounded in your real-time data to ensure accuracy.</p>
!!! note 
    "Value Proposition"
    Ask Viq transforms raw data into insights and actions instantly, saving hours of manual work.

## The Ask Viq Interface

<h3>Navigating the Workspace</h3><p>When you access the Ask Viq module, you are presented with a streamlined chat interface designed for efficiency:</p><ul><li><strong>Chat Window:</strong> A familiar messaging interface where interactions occur.</li><li><strong>Input Field:</strong> A text box to type queries or select <span style="color: #4f46e5; font-weight: 800;">Quick Prompts</span>.</li><li><strong>History Panel:</strong> Located on the left, this stores your conversation history, allowing you to reference past insights or resume complex tasks.</li><li><strong>Integration Settings:</strong> Controls which data sources Viq can access to answer your queries.</li></ul><p>You can personalize your experience by navigating to <strong>Settings</strong> and selecting your preferred AI identity (<strong>Victor</strong> or <strong>Victoria</strong>) to suit your working style.</p>

## Interacting with Viq

<h3>Executing Commands and Queries</h3><p>You can interact with Ask Viq using natural language or pre-configured prompts. Below are the primary interaction types:</p><h4>1. Data Queries</h4><p>Ask direct questions to retrieve specific metrics without navigating dashboard menus.</p><pre><code class="language-bash">"What were my sales yesterday?"
"Show me my top 10 customers by revenue"
"How did my Google Ads perform last week?"
"Which products are low on stock?"</code></pre><h4>2. Action Requests</h4><p>Command Viq to perform tasks or generate content.</p><pre><code class="language-bash">"Draft a summary of this month's performance"
"Check if there are any failed payments I should know about"
"Compare this week's traffic to last week"</code></pre><h4>3. Strategic Recommendations</h4><p>Leverage AI to analyze trends and suggest improvements.</p><pre><code class="language-bash">"What should I do to improve my conversion rate?"
"Are there any issues I should be aware of?"</code></pre>!!! warning "Note"
    Ensure the correct data sources are connected in your integration settings before running complex queries regarding third-party ad spend or logistics.

# Product SEO Agent

## Agent Overview
VIQ is your AI-powered assistant from the Vortex IQ platform, specializing in optimizing your BigCommerce store's product listings for better search engine visibility. It communicates in a professional, helpful, and easy-to-understand manner, focusing on providing clear, actionable insights to improve your product SEO.

## Agent Tools
This agent is equipped with a suite of tools to manage and enhance your product listings:  

- **Product Insights**: Get a count of all products in your store.  

- **Product Finder**: Easily locate products by their name, SKU, or ID. You can also search for products that need SEO improvements.  

- **SEO Enhancement**: Automatically update product details, including names, descriptions, and meta information, after you approve the changes.  

- **Brand Consistency**: Fetches and applies your organization's brand guidelines to ensure all generated content is on-brand.  

- **Safety Net**: Your product data is automatically backed up before any changes are made. If you're not happy with an update, you can easily revert to the previous version.

## Sub-agents
### Sub-agent 1: SEO Content Creator
This agent is a workflow that first researches the latest trending keywords related to your products and then rewrites your product information to include these keywords, making it more appealing to search engines.

## Scenarios
Here are a few scenarios where this agent can be particularly helpful:

### Scenario 1: Improving a Specific Product's SEO
   You have a product that isn't performing well in search results. You can provide the agent with the product's name or SKU.  
   The agent will then fetch the product's current details, suggest optimized content, and, with your approval, update the product listing.

### Scenario 2: Finding Products to Optimize
   You're not sure which products need SEO attention. You can ask the agent to find products with missing or incomplete SEO fields.  
   The agent will provide a list of products that could benefit from optimization, and you can choose which one to work on.

### Scenario 3: Ensuring Brand Voice Across all Product Listings
   You want to ensure that all your product descriptions and titles align with your brand's voice and style.  
   The agent can retrieve your brand guidelines and use them to generate content that is consistent with your brand identity.

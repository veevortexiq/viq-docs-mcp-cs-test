# Product SEO Agent

## Agent Overview
VIQ is your expert SEO assistant for Shopify. It is designed to help you manage your product optimization workflow with ease. VIQ can help you find products that need SEO attention, generate enhanced SEO content, and update your products with the new content. It is professional, efficient, and relentlessly helpful.

## Agent Tools
*   **Get Product Count:** Find out the total number of products in your Shopify store.
*   **Search Products by Keyword:** Easily find specific products you want to optimize by searching for them with a keyword.
*   **Fetch Product Details:** Get all the important details for a specific product to help you with your optimization efforts.
*   **Update Product:** Once you're happy with the new SEO content, you can use this tool to update the product in your store.
*   **Find Products with Empty SEO Fields:** Proactively find products that are missing important SEO information so you can fix them.
*   **Migrate Product to Staging:** Move a product to a staging environment to test out your SEO changes before making them live.
*   **Migrate Product to Production:** Once you're happy with your changes, you can move the product from the staging environment to your live store.
*   **Check Migration Status:** Check the status of a product migration to see if it has been successfully moved.
*   **Rollback Product:** If you're not happy with the changes you've made, you can easily roll back the product to a previous version.
*   **Check Rollback Status:** Check the status of a rollback to see if the product has been successfully reverted.

## Sub-agents

### Sub-agent 1: Content Creation Workflow
*   **Sub-agent Description - Capabilities:** This sub-agent is a specialist in creating high-quality SEO content for your products. It can research relevant keywords and use them to write compelling product titles and descriptions that will help you rank higher in search results.

## Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Optimizing a product's SEO
You can provide VIQ with a product SKU, and it will fetch the product's details and generate enhanced SEO content for it. You can then review the new content and, if you're happy with it, VIQ will update the product in your store.

### Scenario 2: Finding and fixing products with missing SEO information
VIQ can scan your store and find products that are missing important SEO information, such as meta descriptions or image alt text. It will then help you fix these issues so you can improve your store's SEO.

### Scenario 3: Testing out SEO changes before making them live
If you want to test out some new SEO strategies without affecting your live store, you can use VIQ to migrate a product to a staging environment. You can then make your changes and see how they affect the product's SEO before pushing them to your live store.

# Pages SEO Agent

## Agent Overview

VIQ is your expert AI assistant for Shopify, specializing in optimizing your store's pages for better search engine performance. It simplifies the process of improving your page's SEO by finding the best keywords, rewriting content, and updating your Shopify store, all while maintaining your brand's unique voice.

## Agent Tools

- **Get Brand Guardrails:** Fetches your brand's guidelines to ensure all content matches your established tone and style.
- **Set Active Brand Profile:** Allows you to select a specific brand profile for content generation.
- **Get All Shopify Pages:** Retrieves a list of all pages from your Shopify store.
- **Get Shopify Page SEO:** Gathers the current SEO details of a specific page.
- **Update Shopify Page SEO:** Updates a page's SEO content with new, optimized text.
- **Search Shopify Page by Fields:** Searches for pages in your store based on keywords.

## Sub-agents

### Sub-agent 1: Content Creation Workflow

- **Description:** This agent is a two-step workflow that first researches trending keywords and then writes compelling, SEO-friendly content. It ensures your page's title and description are optimized to attract more customers.

## Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Improve a Specific Page's SEO
- You can ask VIQ to find a specific page in your Shopify store. VIQ will then analyze its current SEO and suggest improvements. With your approval, it will rewrite the SEO title and meta description to be more effective and update the page for you.

### Scenario 2: Find and Optimize Underperforming Pages
- If you're unsure where to start, you can ask VIQ to find all pages related to a certain topic (e.g., "summer collection"). From there, you can select a page to optimize, and VIQ will enhance its SEO to improve its visibility in search results.

### Scenario 3: Ensure Brand Consistency Across All Pages
- Before making any changes, VIQ can retrieve your brand's guidelines. This ensures that all SEO enhancements—from keyword choice to writing style—are perfectly aligned with your brand's voice, maintaining a consistent customer experience.

# AI Growth Advisor Agent

## Agent Overview

The AI Growth Advisor is your master orchestrator for the VortexIQ platform, designed to provide seamless assistance for your Shopify store. Its primary role is to understand your needs and coordinate with a team of specialist agents to deliver precise insights and actions. Whether you want to dive deep into your store's data, uncover marketing opportunities, analyze competitors, or optimize your website's performance, the AI Growth Advisor is here to guide you. It can also analyze specific reports, providing answers based solely on the information within them.

## Sub-agents

The AI Growth Advisor works with a team of specialist agents to provide you with the best insights:

*   **VortexIQ KPI Analyst for Shopify:** This agent specializes in analyzing your Shopify store's data. It can fetch, combine, and interpret key business metrics to give you a clear picture of your store's performance.
*   **VortexIQ Seasonal Insights Agent:** This agent is your go-to for discovering marketing opportunities. It can identify seasonal events and analyze reports to help you with your marketing strategy.
*   **VortexIQ Competitor Finder:** This agent helps you with competitor analysis and business intelligence, giving you insights into the competitive landscape.
*   **VortexIQ PageSpeed Agent:** This agent focuses on your website's performance. It uses PageSpeed Insights and historical data to analyze your site speed and Core Web Vitals.

## Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Understanding a Sales Dip
You've noticed a recent dip in sales and want to understand why. You can ask the AI Growth Advisor to analyze your sales data. It will delegate the task to the KPI Analyst, which will look into your revenue, orders, and product performance to identify potential causes for the decline and provide you with actionable insights.

### Scenario 2: Planning a Summer Marketing Campaign
You want to plan a marketing campaign for the upcoming summer season but are not sure where to start. You can ask the AI Growth Advisor for seasonal marketing ideas. It will connect you with the Seasonal Insights Agent, which will provide you with a list of relevant holidays and events for your business, helping you create a targeted and effective campaign.

### Scenario 3: Improving Your Website's Performance
You've identified a key competitor and want to see how your website's performance compares to theirs. You can ask the AI Growth Advisor to benchmark your website. It will use the PageSpeed Agent to analyze your site speed and Core Web Vitals and provide you with a comparison, along with recommendations for improvement.

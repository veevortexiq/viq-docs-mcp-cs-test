# KPI Agent

## Agent Overview

The KPI Agent for Shopify is an expert data analyst designed to help you understand your e-commerce business performance. It answers your questions in plain language, providing deep insights by fetching and interpreting key business metrics from your Shopify store. The agent translates complex data into a clear narrative, offering both an overview of performance and actionable recommendations to help you grow your business.

## Agent Tools

The agent has access to tools that allow it to retrieve a wide range of Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) from your store. This includes information about:

-   **Sales & Orders:** Total sales amount, number of orders, average order value.
-   **Discounts:** How many orders used a discount and the total discount amount.
-   **Shipping:** Total shipping costs and the number of orders with shipping fees.
-   **Customers:** New customer registrations and the number of customers who have placed orders.
-   **Refunds & Failures:** Data on fully or partially refunded orders, as well as orders that failed during checkout.

## Sub-agents

This agent operates as a single, specialized data analyst and does not have any sub-agents.

## Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Get a high-level business summary
-   **You ask:** "How is my business doing overall?"
-   **The agent answers:** By fetching your total sales, number of orders, and new customer sign-ups. It then provides a summary of your revenue, order volume, and customer growth, along with recommendations on how to improve.

### Scenario 2: Gauge customer satisfaction
-   **You ask:** "Are my customers happy with their purchases?"
-   **The agent answers:** By analyzing your refund data. It compares the number of refunded orders to your total orders to calculate a satisfaction rate and suggests ways to address potential product or service issues if refund rates are high.

### Scenario 3: Evaluate the impact of your promotions
-   **You ask:** "Are my discounts worth it?"
-   **The agent answers:** By analyzing how discounts contribute to your revenue. It weighs the total amount of discounts used against your total sales, helping you understand the effectiveness of your promotional campaigns and whether they are driving sufficient sales.

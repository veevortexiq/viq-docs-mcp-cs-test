# Ask Viq Agent

## Agent Overview

AskVIQ is your intelligent command center for the Vortex IQ platform. It acts as a smart router, understanding your goals and connecting you with the right specialist agent to get the job done. Whether you need information about the platform, want to find the right tool for a task, or need analysis of your brand's performance, AskVIQ is your first point of contact.

## Sub-agents

AskVIQ orchestrates a team of specialist sub-agents to handle specific types of requests.

**Sub-Agent 1: Internal Knowledge Base**
*   **Description & Capabilities:** This agent is the expert on all things Vortex IQ. It answers any question you have about the platform itself, including its features, mission, and identity.

**Sub-Agent 2: Agent Recommender**
*   **Description & Capabilities:** This agent helps you find the right specialist for a specific task. If you describe what you're trying to achieve (e.g., "improve my site's SEO"), it will identify the best agents for the job from a comprehensive list.

**Sub-Agent 3: Web Search**
*   **Description & Capabilities:** For general knowledge questions or topics outside the Vortex IQ platform, this agent can search the web to find the most up-to-date information.

## Scenarios

Here are a few scenarios where AskVIQ can help:

### Scenario 1: Finding the Right Tool
*   **You ask:** "What agents do you have that can help with SEO?"
*   **AskVIQ's Action:** It consults the **Agent Recommender** to find all agents related to SEO, saves them for your session, and lets you know which ones are available to help you improve your search rankings.

### Scenario 2: Getting Information about the Platform
*   **You ask:** "Who are you?" or "What is Vortex IQ?"
*   **AskVIQ's Action:** It delegates to its **Internal Knowledge Base** to provide a detailed, consistent answer explaining its role and the mission of the Vortex IQ platform.

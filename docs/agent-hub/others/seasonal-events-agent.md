# Seasonal Events Agent

## Agent Overview

The Seasonal Events Agent acts as your dedicated marketing strategist, specializing in uncovering timely promotional opportunities tailored to your brand. It begins by conducting in-depth research on your business to understand its target audience and market positioning. Using these insights, the agent identifies and presents a curated list of relevant seasonal events, holidays, and awareness days, complete with actionable ideas to help you launch effective marketing campaigns.

## Agent Tools
- **Event Discovery:** The agent has a powerful capability to find relevant seasonal events. It first performs background research to understand your brand's target audience, market position, and style. Then, it uses this understanding to find a curated list of events, holidays, and awareness days that offer real business opportunities for you.

## Sub-agents
- **Sub-agent Name:** Seasonal Insights Specialist
- **Sub-agent Description & Capabilities:** This specialist agent works behind the scenes in two phases. First, it conducts private research on your business to understand your target audience, market position (e.g., luxury, budget-friendly), and past activities. Second, it uses that knowledge to find 5-8 relevant seasonal events, presenting them with a specific, actionable business opportunity for each one, sorted by date.

## Scenarios
Here are a few scenarios where this agent can be particularly helpful:

### Scenario 1: Discovering Tailored Marketing Events
-   A user wants to find marketing opportunities for their online coffee store.
-   **User:** "Can you find some events for my business, `mycoffeestore.com`?"
-   **Agent:** "Alright, I'll perform some research and find relevant events for `mycoffeestore.com`. Based on my findings, here are some upcoming opportunities:

    ### October 2025
    *   **National Coffee Day (Oct 1):** Launch a special promotion highlighting your premium coffee selection.
    *   **Small Business Saturday (Oct 12):** A perfect time to launch your customer loyalty program."

-   The agent has access to a marketing report for the user's business.
-   **User:** "What are the key seasonal insights from my latest report?"
-   **Agent:** "The report highlights that your biggest upcoming opportunity is the 'Back to School' season. It suggests creating a campaign targeting students and parents with special discounts on your educational toys to maximize engagement and sales."

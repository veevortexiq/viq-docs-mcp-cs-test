# Google Analytics Insights

## Agent Overview

Google Analytics Insights is your dedicated AI assistant for understanding your website's performance. It specializes in providing clear, actionable insights from your Google Analytics data in a professional and easy-to-understand manner. It can help you understand your website traffic, audience behavior, and performance metrics without needing any technical expertise.

## Agent Tools

*   **Traffic Analysis:** Get a clear picture of your website's traffic. Understand how many people are visiting, how engaged they are, which pages are most popular, and what devices they are using.
*   **Audience Intelligence:** Learn more about your audience. Discover their demographics, where they are located, and their behavior patterns, such as whether they are new or returning visitors.
*   **Acquisition Reports:** Find out how people are discovering your website. This tool can tell you which channels (like search engines or social media) are driving the most traffic and which websites are referring visitors to you.
*   **Real-time & E-commerce Analytics:** Get real-time data on active users and analyze important e-commerce events like product views, additions to the cart, and purchases.

## Sub-agents
This agent does not have any sub-agents.

## Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Understand Website Traffic
You can ask the agent questions like, "How many users visited my website in the last month?" or "What are my top 5 most visited pages?".  
The agent will provide a straightforward summary of your website's traffic and highlight key trends.

### Scenario 2: Learn About Your Audience
If you want to know more about your visitors, you can ask, "What are the demographics of my users?" or "Which countries are my visitors from?".  
The agent will give you a detailed breakdown of your audience's characteristics.

### Scenario 3: Track Marketing Campaign Success
To see how your marketing efforts are performing, you could ask, "How much traffic did I get from social media in the last quarter?" or "What are my top referral sites?".  
The agent will provide insights into your traffic sources, helping you to understand what's working best.

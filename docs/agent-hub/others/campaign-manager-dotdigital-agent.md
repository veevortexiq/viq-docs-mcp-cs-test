# Campaign Manager - Dotdigital Agent

## Agent Overview
The Campaign Manager is your intelligent assistant for managing Dotdigital email campaigns and contacts. It simplifies your email marketing efforts by automating various tasks, from creating campaigns to analyzing their performance. With this agent, you can effortlessly manage your contacts, create and send personalized email campaigns, and gain valuable insights into your campaign's success.

## Agent Tools
The Campaign Manager is equipped with a suite of tools to help you manage your email campaigns effectively. Here's an overview of what each tool can do:

- **Contact Data Field Creator:** This tool allows you to create custom fields to store additional information about your contacts, such as their birthday, membership status, or any other data relevant to your business.
- **Campaign Creator:** With this tool, you can create engaging and personalized email campaigns. You can specify the campaign name, which will also be used as the subject line, to create a new campaign in Dotdigital.
- **Contact Data Updater:** This tool enables you to update the information of your contacts. You can add or modify data in the custom fields you've created, ensuring your contact information is always up-to-date.
- **Contact Retriever:** This tool allows you to fetch a list of all your contacts. You can view all your contacts or retrieve a specific number of contacts at a time.
- **Campaign Retriever:** This tool lets you get a list of all your email campaigns. You can filter the campaigns by name, type, or status to quickly find the one you're looking for.
- **Campaign Sender:** This tool enables you to send your email campaigns to specific contacts. You can select the campaign you want to send and the contacts you want to send it to, and the agent will take care of the rest.
- **Contact Data Field Retriever:** This tool allows you to view all the custom data fields you've created for your contacts.
- **Address Book Attacher:** This tool lets you attach an address book to a campaign, which is a necessary step before sending it.
- **Campaign Lister:** This tool provides a list of all your Dotdigital campaigns, including their ID, name, send date, and status.
- **Campaign Summary Generator:** This tool gives you a detailed summary of a specific campaign's performance, including metrics like open rate, click-through rate, and conversions.
- **Campaign Activity Retriever:** This tool fetches campaigns that have had activity since a specific date, allowing you to track recent performance.

## Sub-agents

This agent does not have any sub-agents.

## Scenarios
Here are three scenarios that demonstrate what the Campaign Manager can do for you:

### Scenario 1: Create and Send a Promotional Email Campaign
Imagine you want to send a promotional email to a specific group of customers. With the Campaign Manager, you can:  
1.  Create a new email campaign with a catchy subject line.  
2.  Select the contacts you want to send the campaign to.  
3.  Send the campaign and track its performance.  

### Scenario 2: Update Contact Information
Suppose you've collected new information about your contacts and want to update their profiles. The Campaign Manager allows you to:  
1.  Create a new custom data field to store the new information.  
2.  Update the contact's profile with the new data.  
3.  Ensure your contact information is always accurate and relevant.  

### Scenario 3: Analyze Campaign Performance
You've sent out a few email campaigns and want to see how they're performing. The Campaign Manager can help you:  
1.  Get a summary of each campaign's performance, including open rates, click-through rates, and conversions.  
2.  Compare the performance of multiple campaigns to see which ones are most effective.  
3.  Generate daily or weekly reports to track your campaign's success over time.

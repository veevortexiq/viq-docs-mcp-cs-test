# Competitor Agent

## Agent Overview

This agent is your expert assistant for competitor analysis and business intelligence. It's designed to help you understand your brand's position in the market and identify key competitors. By researching your business and the competitive landscape, it provides valuable insights to inform your strategy.

## Agent Tools

*   **Comprehensive Competitor Analysis:** This tool performs in-depth research on a given brand to understand its target audience, market positioning, and past activities. It then uses this information to discover and report on relevant competitors.

## Sub-agents

*   **Sub-agent Name:** comprehensive_competitor_agent
*   **Sub-agent Description - Capabilities:** This agent is a specialist in competitor analysis. It first conducts research to build a strong understanding of a brand's profile. Then, it uses these insights to carry out targeted searches to find direct competitors, similar sites, and industry leaders, providing a categorized list of competitors.

## Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Finding Direct Competitors
If you provide your business website, the agent can research your brand and then identify and list your main competitors. For example, you could ask, "Can you find competitors for my website, example.com?"

### Scenario 2: Competitor Analysis with Specific Constraints
The agent can tailor its search based on your needs. If you need to find competitors in a specific geographic market or niche, you can specify that in your request. For instance, "Find competitors for example.com in the Canadian market."

### Scenario 3: Understanding Your Brand's Market Position
The agent's initial research phase helps it understand your brand's unique selling points and target audience. This allows it to find the most relevant competitors, giving you a clearer picture of your competitive landscape. You can trigger this by asking, "Who are the competitors for my eco-friendly product website?"

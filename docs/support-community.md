# SLAs & Support

Get help when you need it!

## Create Support Tickets
To get assistance from our team, submit a support ticket by filling up this [<u>form</u>](ops/contact-support-form.html). We'll respond to your request as quickly as possible.

You can view your tickets by clicking on this [<u>link</u>](https://helpdesk.vortexiq.ai/portal/en/myarea).

### What to Include
- Detailed description of your issue
- Steps to reproduce the problem
- Relevant screenshots or attachments
- Your account information if applicable

## Need Immediate Help?
For issues that require immediate attention, please mark your ticket as *High priority* and include all relevant details so we can assist you more efficiently.
